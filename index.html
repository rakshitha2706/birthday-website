To My Dearest Bestie 💌

From the very first "hello" back in intermediate college, I never imagined we'd become this close. We were strangers then — focused on studies, barely knowing each other — but somehow, our paths kept crossing, and I'm so grateful they did.

You became someone I could trust, laugh with, cry to, and share every little thing with. You became my best bestie. 🫂

You're like a burst of morning sunshine — always bright, full of energy, and ready to explore life. And then there's me, your night owl bestie, probably still wide awake while you're already dreaming by 2 or 3 AM. But somehow, we balance each other perfectly.

Our outings are always my favorite — from collecting giant leaves in the park to painting them like artists on a mission, to every little game we played and ending it with an ice cream treat. Moments with you are like tiny treasures I never want to lose.

I've seen you grow into this multi-talented person who dances with her heart, writes with her soul, learns everything she sets her mind to, and always inspires the people around her. From paper quilling to amazing crafts, poetry which melts our hearts, with connective words — there's nothing you can't do.

You've been my light in the low times, the one who gives me hope, who listens when no one else does, who encourages me to dream and try. Being your friend is one of the best things that ever happened to me.

I like you so, so much, Oditi. You're my bestie forever, and no matter where life takes us, you'll always have a special place within me.

Happy Birthday, Oditi. Keep shining, keep dancing, and keep being the beautiful soul that you are.

— Yours always,
Rakshitha 💕
